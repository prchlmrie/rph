Analysis of Diego Silang's Letter

Silang's convictions did not possess an anti-colonial character

Sacrifice

In the process of going against the Spaniards, he was willing to make himself and his province subject to another colonial power.

What we need to understand about Silang's context

Idea

The idea of Nation was still nonexistent in 1762. Silang's imagination was limited to his locality

Demand

Silang's demand was anchored on practical matters and not on some ideals. (Burden to the common masses)

Revolt

Revolts that took place during the Spanish period were not always anti-colonial. They rebelled against the abuses but have not thought of a future without a colonial power.
